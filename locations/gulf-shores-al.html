UV-Resistant Coatings for Gulf Shores Sun

Gulf Shores averages over 220 sunny days per year, and that intense Alabama Gulf Coast sun can destroy inferior floor coatings. Yellowing, fading, and chalking are common problems with cheap epoxy products.

Our polyaspartic coatings are 100% UV-stable. They won't yellow, fade, or chalk—even after years of direct sun exposure. Your Gulf Shores garage floor will look as vibrant and beautiful years from now as the day it was installed.

This UV resistance is especially important for Gulf Shores homes where garages often serve as multi-purpose spaces with doors frequently left open to enjoy the coastal breeze.
