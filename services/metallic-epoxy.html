Metallic Epoxy: Where Durability Meets Design

Metallic epoxy flooring creates a breathtaking, three-dimensional effect that looks like liquid metal, flowing lava, or ocean waves frozen in resin. Each floor is completely unique — no two installations ever look the same.

The secret is in metallic pigments suspended in high-performance epoxy resin. As the coating is applied, our installers manipulate the pigments to create flowing, swirling patterns with incredible depth and movement. The result is a floor that stops people in their tracks.

But metallic epoxy is not just beautiful — it is built on the same industrial-grade epoxy chemistry that protects warehouse floors. You get a seamless, easy-to-clean surface that resists stains, chemicals, and abrasion while looking absolutely stunning.

The Metallic Process

Each metallic floor requires careful preparation and skilled application. We begin with thorough concrete preparation including diamond grinding and repair work. A primer coat ensures proper adhesion. Then, the metallic epoxy is applied and artistically manipulated using specialized techniques to create movement and depth in the pigments. Finally, a high-gloss clear coat seals everything in, creating a mirror-like finish with incredible durability.

Metallic Effects Available

Luminous, pearlescent white with soft silver undertones. Creates a bright, open feel with subtle shimmer and elegant movement.

Ocean Blue
