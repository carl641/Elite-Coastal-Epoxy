Outdoor Floors That Beat the Alabama Sun

UV-stable, slip-resistant coatings for patios, pool decks, porches, and walkways

Outdoor Spaces Deserve Protection Too

Baldwin County's outdoor surfaces face a unique combination of intense UV exposure, salt air, heavy rain, and extreme humidity. Standard coatings crack, peel, and fade within a year. Our outdoor-rated systems are formulated specifically for Gulf Coast conditions.

Whether you are coating a backyard patio, a pool deck at your Orange Beach condo, or a commercial walkway, we use polyaspartic and polyurea systems that resist fading, chalking, and delamination in direct sunlight and coastal weather.

Applications

  • Patios and covered porches
  • Pool decks and surrounds
  • Driveways and walkways
  • Outdoor kitchen and entertaining areas
  • Condo and apartment breezeways
  • Commercial entryways and sidewalks

Key Benefits

UV Stable

Won't yellow, chalk, or fade in direct Alabama sunlight. Looks fresh year after year.

Slip Resistant

Textured finish keeps surfaces safe when wet — critical for pool decks and rain-exposed areas.

Temperature Tolerant

Handles extreme heat in summer and cold snaps in winter without cracking or peeling.

Easy Maintenance

Hose it off, sweep it clean. The sealed surface resists mold, mildew, and staining.

Color Options

Wide range of colors and textures including cool-touch finishes that stay comfortable barefoot.

Our Installation Process

Every outdoor project follows a proven five-step process for lasting results

1

Inspect

We assess the concrete condition, drainage, sun exposure, and identify any repairs needed before coating.

2

Prepare

Diamond grinding and shot blasting open up the concrete pores for maximum adhesion in outdoor conditions.

3

Repair

Cracks, spalls, and uneven areas are filled and leveled. Expansion joints are properly addressed.

4

Coat

UV-stable polyaspartic base coat is applied, followed by decorative broadcast and anti-slip additives.

5

Seal

A clear UV-resistant top coat locks everything in and provides long-term protection against the elements.
