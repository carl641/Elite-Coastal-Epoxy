Fix the Foundation Before You Coat

Professional concrete repair and surface preparation for lasting epoxy and coating results

The Most Important Step in Any Coating Job

Here's the truth most contractors won't tell you: the coating you put on your floor is only as good as the concrete underneath it. Cracks, spalls, moisture issues, and poorly prepared surfaces are the number one reason coatings fail — not the product itself.

At Elite Coastal Epoxy, we never skip the prep work. In fact, we spend more time preparing your concrete than actually applying the coating. That is what separates a floor that lasts a decade from one that peels in a year.

Whether your concrete needs minor crack repair or major rehabilitation, we have the tools, materials, and experience to get it right. And if your floor has a failed DIY coating or a competitor's product that did not hold up, we can remove it and start fresh.

Our Repair Services

  • Crack Filling — Structural and cosmetic crack repair using flexible epoxy and polyurea fillers that move with the concrete
  • Spall Repair — Patching and rebuilding chipped, flaking, and deteriorated concrete surfaces back to a smooth, level finish
  • Concrete Leveling — Grinding and self-leveling compounds to eliminate trip hazards, uneven surfaces, and low spots
  • Expansion Joint Treatment — Proper filling and sealing of control joints and expansion joints to prevent future cracking and coating failure
  • Moisture Barrier Installation — Vapor-blocking epoxy for slabs with high moisture content, critical in Baldwin County's humid climate
  • Previous Coating Removal — Diamond grinding, shot blasting, and chemical stripping to remove failed paint, epoxy, or sealers

Surface Preparation Methods

We use professional-grade equipment to prepare every square inch of your floor

Diamond Grinding

Industrial diamond grinders remove the top layer of concrete, opening up the pores for maximum coating adhesion. This creates a consistent surface profile across the entire floor.

Shot Blasting

Steel shot is propelled at high velocity against the concrete surface, creating an aggressive profile ideal for thick-build systems. Excellent for removing old coatings and contamination.

Moisture Testing

We perform calcium chloride and relative humidity tests to measure moisture levels in your slab. High moisture requires a vapor barrier epoxy before any coating can be applied.
