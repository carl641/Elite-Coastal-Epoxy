Garage Floor Coatings That Actually Last

Professional-grade epoxy and polyaspartic coatings for Baldwin County homeowners

Transform Your Garage Into a Showroom

Your garage floor takes a beating every single day — hot tires, oil drips, road salt, heavy tools, and Alabama humidity. Our residential garage floor coatings are engineered to handle all of it while looking incredible for years to come.

We use a multi-layer system that bonds directly to your concrete, creating a surface that is stronger, more durable, and far more attractive than bare concrete or DIY kits. Every installation is backed by our satisfaction guarantee and designed specifically for the Gulf Coast climate.

What's Included

  • Diamond grinding surface preparation
  • Crack and spall repair as needed
  • Moisture-tolerant epoxy coat
  • High-build MVB epoxy base coat
  • Decorative flake or stain application
  • UV-stable polyaspartic clear top coat

Perfect For

  • Single, double, and triple-car garages
  • Workshop and hobby spaces
  • Home gym floors
  • Laundry rooms and utility areas
  • Attached garage living conversions
