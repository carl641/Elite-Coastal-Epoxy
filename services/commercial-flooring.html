Commercial Flooring Systems

We match the right system to your specific industry requirements

Epoxy Mortar Systems

The toughest flooring system available. Ideal for warehouses, manufacturing facilities, and heavy equipment areas. Handles extreme impact, heavy loads, and forklift traffic without cracking or chipping.

Best for: Warehouses, factories, loading docks

Quartz-Filled Epoxy

Combines durability with a decorative, slip-resistant finish. The quartz broadcast creates excellent traction in wet environments while maintaining a clean, professional appearance.

Best for: Restaurants, hospitals, locker rooms

ESD (Electrostatic Dissipative)

Specialized flooring that safely dissipates static electricity. Critical for electronics manufacturing, server rooms, and any environment where static discharge can damage sensitive equipment.

Best for: Server rooms, electronics labs, clean rooms

Decorative Flake Systems

Full-broadcast vinyl flake systems that deliver both beauty and performance. Available in dozens of color combinations to match your brand or décor while providing a durable, seamless surface.

Best for: Retail, showrooms, offices, gyms
