Your Local Baldwin County Floor Coating Experts

Family-owned. Gulf Coast tough. Customer-obsessed.

From One Garage to 500+ Floors Across Baldwin County

Elite Coastal Epoxy was founded in 2015 after we saw the same story repeat itself across Baldwin County: homeowners investing in cheap, one-day epoxy coatings only to watch them peel, bubble, and fail within months. The Gulf Coast climate — with its relentless humidity, salt air, and temperature swings — demands more than a hardware-store kit or a franchise crew passing through town.

We decided to build the company differently. We committed to premium polyaspartic and industrial-grade epoxy systems specifically engineered for coastal environments. We invested in professional diamond-grinding equipment for meticulous surface preparation — the step most competitors skip but the one that determines whether a floor lasts two years or twenty.

Every crew member is local to Baldwin County. They understand the soil conditions, the moisture challenges, and the standards our neighbors expect. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees, honest timelines, and communication you can actually count on.

Over the past decade, we have completed more than 500 floors across Daphne, Fairhope, Foley, Gulf Shores, and Orange Beach — from two-car garages to 20,000-square-foot commercial warehouses. Each one was prepped, primed, and coated as if it were our own home. That is the standard we set on day one, and it is the standard we hold today.
