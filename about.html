Your Local Baldwin County Floor Coating Team

Elite Coastal Epoxy was founded in 2023 after we saw the same story repeat itself across Baldwin County: homeowners investing in cheap, one-day epoxy coatings only to watch them peel, bubble, and fail within months. The Gulf Coast climate — with its relentless humidity, salt air, and temperature swings — demands more than a hardware-store kit or a franchise crew passing through town.

We decided to build the company differently. We committed to premium polyaspartic and industrial-grade epoxy systems specifically engineered for coastal environments. We invested in professional diamond-grinding equipment for meticulous surface preparation — the step most competitors skip but the one that determines whether a floor lasts two years or twenty.

Every crew member is local to Baldwin County. They understand the soil conditions, the moisture challenges, and the standards our neighbors expect. We believe in honest timelines and communication you can actually count on.

We have completed floors across Fairhope, Spanish Fort, Daphne, Orange Beach, Gulf Shores, and beyond — from two-car garages to commercial warehouses. Each one was prepped and coated as if it were our own home. That is the standard we set on day one, and it is the standard we hold today.
