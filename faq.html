Product & Durability

For the Gulf Coast climate, polyaspartic coatings offer several advantages over traditional epoxy. They provide superior UV resistance so they will not yellow or fade in the Alabama sun. They cure faster, allowing same-day return to service. They are more flexible, which helps them handle the expansion and contraction caused by our temperature swings. We often recommend polyaspartic as a topcoat over an epoxy base for the best combination of durability and performance.

With proper installation and basic maintenance, our floor coatings last 15-20 years or more. The exact lifespan depends on the level of traffic, the coating system used, and how well the floor is maintained. Our 20-year warranty ensures you are covered.

Existing paint or coatings must be removed first to ensure proper adhesion. Applying a new coating over an old one creates a weak bond that will eventually fail. We handle the full removal and surface preparation as part of our process, so you do not need to worry about doing any prep work yourself.

Maintenance

Maintenance is simple. Sweep or dust mop regularly to remove dirt and debris, and mop with warm water and a mild cleaner as needed. No special products or expensive treatments are required. Your coated floor is designed to be low-maintenance and easy to keep clean, which is one of its biggest benefits over bare concrete.

Most common household and automotive chemicals are perfectly safe on our coatings, including brake fluid, oil, gasoline, and most cleaning products. We provide a detailed care guide with every completed project so you know exactly what works best for your specific coating system.
