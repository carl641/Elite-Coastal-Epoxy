At Elite Coastal Epoxy, we specialize in high-performance floor coatings featuring an MVB base of epoxy with a polyaspartic top coat, designed specifically for the unique demands of Baldwin County's Gulf Coast climate. Our Proven 4-Step Process

Every floor gets the same professional-grade treatment

1

Diamond Grind Preparation

We use industrial diamond grinders to profile your concrete, removing contaminants and creating the ideal surface for a permanent mechanical bond. No shortcuts, no acid etching.

2

Repair & Seal

Cracks, spalls, and imperfections are chased, filled, and repaired. An epoxy is applied to seal moisture and concrete, providing the foundation for our coating system.

3

Decorative Flake or Stain Application

Your chosen decorative flake or stain is applied to the base coat for a seamless, slip-resistant finish with depth and character unique to your floor.

4

Clear Polyaspartic Topcoat

A UV-stable, high-gloss polyaspartic topcoat seals everything in. This final layer provides chemical resistance, hot tire resistance, and a finish that stays beautiful for years.
